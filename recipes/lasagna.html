This lasagna recipe is the only one you'll need to create a delicious homemade dish. Layered with pasta, rich tomato sauce, savory meat or vegetables, and melted cheese, it's a comforting meal that can be enjoyed for dinner or as leftovers the next day.

Ingredients

  • Meat: This lasagna features a combination of sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • Onion and garlic: Cooked with the meat, an onion and two cloves of garlic add flavorful depth to the dish
  • Tomato products: The sauce is made with crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar subtly enhance the sauce's flavor with a touch of sweetness
  • Spices and seasonings:u Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, salt, and black pepper provide delightful seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles: Choose between store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles for layering
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese combine for a decadently cheesy lasagna
  • Egg: An egg is included in the recipe to bind the ricotta filling, ensuring a neat presentation when sliced

    • Instructions

    Step 1

    Prepare the Flavorful Meat Sauce: Create a mouthwatering meat sauce by combining sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef. Saut√© them with diced onions and minced garlic to infuse the sauce with rich and savory flavors. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste, allowing the sauce to simmer and develop its robust taste. A subtle touch of white sugar harmoniously balances the flavors, resulting in a truly delightful sauce.

    Step 2

    Cook the Perfect Noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles until al dente, achieving the ideal texture for layering. The tender noodles will provide a satisfying bite, ensuring each layer of your lasagna is just right.

    Step 3

    Craft the Creamy Ricotta Mixture: Create a luscious ricotta mixture by combining creamy ricotta cheese with beaten eggs. The addition of eggs helps bind the filling, ensuring it stays intact during baking. This creamy layer adds a luxurious texture and enhances the overall richness of the lasagna.

    Step 4

    Layer with Precision: Assemble your lasagna with precision, following the recipe instructions carefully. Start with a generous layer of the meat sauce, then add a layer of perfectly cooked lasagna noodles. Spread a dollop of the ricotta mixture on top, followed by a sprinkle of grated Parmesan and shredded mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until your baking dish is filled to perfection.

    Step 5

    Bake to Golden Perfection: Cover the assembled lasagna with foil to retain moisture and prevent excessive browning. Bake it in the oven at the specified temperature, allowing the flavors to meld together while the cheese turns golden and bubbly. The aroma that fills your kitchen will surely make your taste buds tingle with anticipation.

    How to Layer Lasagna

    Step 1

    Meat sauce: Prepare a rich and flavorful meat sauce by combining sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef cooked with onions, garlic, and a variety of aromatic spices, creating a savory base that will infuse your lasagna with irresistible taste.

    Step 2

    Noodles: Cook lasagna noodles to perfection, ensuring they are tender yet firm enough to hold the layers together. These perfectly cooked noodles provide a comforting and satisfying texture that complements the other ingredients.

    Step 3

    Ricotta mixture: Whip up a creamy ricotta mixture, combining smooth ricotta cheese with beaten eggs, creating a velvety layer that adds a delightful richness to your lasagna, making every bite a heavenly experience.

    Step 4

    Mozzarella slices: Layer your lasagna with slices of gooey, melted mozzarella cheese, offering a delightful stretchy texture that blankets the meat sauce and creates a delightful contrast to the other components.

    Step 5

    Meat sauce: Continue layering your lasagna with another generous spread of the savory meat sauce, building upon the flavors and ensuring each bite is packed with the robust taste that the sauce brings to the dish.

    Step 6

    Parmesan cheese: Sprinkle the layers with grated Parmesan cheese, adding a nutty and salty note that enhances the overall depth of flavor, creating a beautiful melding of the ingredients.

    Step 7

    Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan: Continue layering the lasagna with the noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, mozzarella slices, and Parmesan cheese, repeating the process until your baking dish is filled to perfection. Finish off with a final sprinkling of Parmesan cheese on top, allowing it to form a delicious golden crust during baking.

    How Long to Cook Lasagna

    Bake the assembled lasagna at 375 degrees F for approximately 50 minutes. Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes, then bake uncovered for the remaining 25 minutes. After removing it from the oven, allow the lasagna to rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before serving.
